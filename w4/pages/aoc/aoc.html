Summary

Anakin is now a padawan being trained by Obi-Wan, but while that is happening the Jedis are struggling to hold control of the republic, and keeping peace of the galaxy. Alot of systems around the galaxy are threating to leave the republic. Anakin is assigned a mission to watch over the senator Padme where they get "closer" which is against Jedi code.. During this Obi-Wan finds out a secret clone army being built for the republic on Kamino. He then discovers the Sith conspicary thats behind most of this infighting in the galaxy. The movie ends with 1000's of systems starting the seperatists, starting the clone wars...

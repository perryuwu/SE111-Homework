Summary

Two Jedi Knights Obi-Wan and Qui-Gon Jinn are tasked to handle a blockade on the planet Naboo from the Trade Federation. While this is all brewing Darth Sidious is behind the scenes controlling events which leads to a battle occuring. After the 2 Jedis escape they make it to Tatooine where they meet a young slave-boy named Anakin Skywalker, who has highest amount of Midichlorians ever and that he may be the chosen one...

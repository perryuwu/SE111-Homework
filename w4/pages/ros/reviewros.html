This may be a slightly biased review, but this is my favorite movie in the world. Anakin being grown up now and being in his secret marriage with Padme, to the Clone Wars now fully in effect it just was so well done. General Grievous having alot of screen time is also amazing, his design his character and him being a coward is just so perfect. Anakin slowly falling for temptation and fear and falling to the dark side is done perfectly, and people want to say his change to the dark side is rushed, but I disagree the Clone War's series has helped alot, and the movie itself seems to do enough to show why he needed to become Darth Vader.

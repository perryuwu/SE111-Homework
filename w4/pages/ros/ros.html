Summary

The galaxy is in full war with the clone wars raging on. The Jedi are trying there hadrest to keep the republic together. While this happening Anakin Skywalker is struggling with his emotions and desire for more power, and when Darth Sidious promises him he can save his secret wife Padme, he falls to the dark side. Where order 66 happens, and most Jedi die accros the galaxy. Obi-Wan, anakins master and mentor, confronts Anakin at Mustafar where they have a lightsaber duel where Anakin loses being severely injured, where he then becomes Darth Vader. The movie ends with the republic falling, and becoming the first Galactic Empire, at the same time Jedi are going in to hiding, and Padme dies in child birth and has twins, Anakins childern, Luke and Leia who will be key players in the orginal three Star War's movies.
